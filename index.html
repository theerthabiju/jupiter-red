About our Company

We focus on delivering high-performance, cost-effective, and sustainable firefighting system installation and maintenance solutions to enhance safety and support the nation’s infrastructure and development goals

Jupiter X Trading Contracting and Services is a Qatar-based company committed to delivering end-to-end solutions across the fire protection and general contracting sectors. Our core expertise lies in the design, supply, installation, testing, and commissioning of a full range of fire protection systems—including Fire Alarms (addressable & conventional), Fire Fighting Systems, Clean Agent and Foam Suppression Systems, Kitchen Hood Systems, Aerosol Suppression, and Passive Fire Protection.
We also specialize in the integration of Emergency Exit Lighting and Central Battery Systems, ensuring safe and compliant evacuation infrastructure. Our services are designed to meet the strict standards of local authorities including QCDD, while also addressing the unique safety and operational needs of each project.

Beyond fire protection, Jupiter X provides comprehensive MEP services and high-quality carpentry works, delivering tailored solutions for commercial, industrial, and residential projects. Every service we offer is rooted in safety, sustainability, and superior execution — supporting our clients’ vision from foundation to finish.

Kitchen Fire System

Fire Doors & Accessories
